## 2. Theme

The Swiss {ai} Weeks are hackathons aimed at turning AI research into real societal and economic impact. Through hackathons, expert events, public programs, and startup support, they bring together researchers, developers, entrepreneurs, and citizens to promote ethical, open, and trustworthy AI.
